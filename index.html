Our Core Project: Smart Farm System

Discover how Smart Farm Future's innovative technology is transforming agriculture.

Soil Monitoring

Sensor Used: RS485-based 7-in-1 soil sensor

Parameters Measured:

  • Humidity
  • Temperature
  • Electrical Conductivity (EC)
  • pH level
  • Nitrogen (N)
  • Phosphorus (P)
  • Potassium (K)

Data Flow:

  1. The ESP32 sends Modbus commands to the soil sensor.
  2. The response is parsed and displayed on an I2C LCD screen.
  3. All values are uploaded to Firebase Realtime Database.

Pest Repellent System

Our Smart Farm provides 24-hour pest protection using light-based methods. It employs an intelligent approach utilizing the behavioral patterns of insects.

üåû Daytime Defense

A Silver reflective film is installed around the crops to reflect sunlight and disorient insects that avoid bright, flickering light. This is an eco-friendly yet effective solution for preventing pest access.



üåô Nighttime Defense

When it gets dark, a tri-color RGB LED system is activated. The color and intensity are optimized to repel nocturnal insects without harming the plants.



üîÑ Automatic Light Detection

A light sensor (photoresistor) monitors ambient brightness. Based on the light level, the system automatically switches between day and night modes without human input, ensuring complete protection.

Wild Animal Detection and Repellent

Our system also protects crops from wild animals using motion-based defense. This significantly helps in reducing crop damage, especially during nighttime or early morning hours.

üì° Ultrasonic Distance Sensor (HC-SR04)

When a wild animal approaches the perimeter, an ultrasonic sensor detects movement or presence instantly. This non-contact sensor continuously monitors for animal intrusions.

üîä Sound-Based Repellent

Upon detection, a buzzer emits a loud, sudden sound to startle and repel intruding animals. This is a humane method that effectively deters animals without causing them harm.

Smart Irrigation System

Actuators Used:

Relay Module (for water pump/valve)

Servo Motor (to rotate valve handle)

Control Logic:

1. The system checks two triggers:

- Soil moisture below 15% triggers automatic irrigation.
- A remote toggle button from the mobile app triggers manual irrigation via Firebase.

2. When triggered:

- The relay is powered ON.
- The servo rotates to simulate a valve opening and closing.
